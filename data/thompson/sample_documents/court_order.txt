The Court, having reviewed the docket in this matter, orders as follows:

1. A Case Management Conference is set for June 18, 2026, at 9:00 AM in
   Department 3. All counsel of record must appear in person; telephonic
   appearance is not permitted absent good cause.

2. Plaintiff shall file a Case Management Statement no later than
   June 8, 2026 — ten (10) calendar days prior to the conference. The
   statement must address: (a) service of process on all named defendants,
   (b) any defendants who must be joined including federal agencies,
   (c) status of settlement discussions, (d) trial readiness estimate,
   and (e) any pending motions.

3. Proof of service on all named defendants, including the Internal
   Revenue Service pursuant to 28 U.S.C. § 2410, must be filed no later
   than June 1, 2026.

4. Counsel for Plaintiff shall produce a payoff statement current within
   thirty (30) days of the conference and lodge it with the Court by
   June 13, 2026.

5. Failure to comply with this order may result in the issuance of an
   Order to Show Cause re: sanctions.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
